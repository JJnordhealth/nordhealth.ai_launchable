The primary source of personal data is information submitted directly by customers and users of Nordhealth Nora. Additional data may be obtained from:

5. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not disclose personal data from our register to external parties except as described below:

  • Trusted subcontractors who process data on our behalf under strict contractual obligations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

For transfers outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including EU Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

6. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data:

  • Access restricted to authorized employees only
  • Protected by firewalls and encryption
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Safe and secure backup procedures
  • Employee training on data protection

7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable legal requirements. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to lodge a complaint: File a complaint with the supervisory authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at dpo@nordhealth.com.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Nordhealth Nora uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze usage patterns, and deliver personalized content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy
